Wattpilot Exporter

A personal dashboard for tracking your EV charging costs, energy consumption, and savings.

📡 What it does

Wattpilot Exporter fetches your EV charging session data from the Wattpilot cloud API and turns it into a clean, easy-to-read dashboard.

It calculates how much energy you consumed each month, the official tax-relevant cost based on Austrian government rates, and your estimated savings when charging from your own solar energy.

📊

Monthly Stats

Browse energy and cost data month by month, with intuitive navigation.

📈

Session Charts

Visualise per-session and historical charging data with interactive bar charts.

💶

Cost Calculation

Uses the official Austrian government kWh rates to compute the exact tax-relevant car benefit (Sachbezug).

☀️

Solar Savings

Tracks estimated savings (margin) when sessions are charged with eco / solar energy.

🚀 Running the app

Set the WATTPILOT_KEY environment variable — you can find it in your Wattpilot export URL as the ?e= parameter.

cd src
go run .

Open http://localhost:8080 in your browser.
